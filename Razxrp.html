Server Guidelines

1. Value Your Life (NVL)

Players must realistically value their characters' lives. If you are cornered or outgunned without a tactical advantage, you must comply with your aggressors.

2. Powergaming & Metagaming

Using outside information that your character wouldn't know (Metagaming) or forcing actions that give other players no mechanical way to counter (Powergaming) is strictly prohibited.

3. Fail RP / Out of Character (OOC)

Stay in character at all times while on the server. If an issue arises, report it via the player dashboard or Discord ticket system instead of breaking roleplay scenarios.
